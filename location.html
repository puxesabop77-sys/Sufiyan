🚌 HOW TO REACH

Getting Here

🚂

By Train

Nearest railway station: Kishanganj Railway Station (12 km). From station, take auto or bus to Bahadurganj. Journey ~25 minutes.

🚌

By Bus

Direct buses available from Kishanganj, Purnia and Siliguri to Bahadurganj Bus Stand. Anti Lodge is 5 min walk from bus stand.

🛺

By Auto

From Bahadurganj Chowk, take auto to Anti Lodge area. Tell the driver 'Talat Welfare Lodge' — everyone knows it locally.

🚗

By Car / Bike

Enter Bahadurganj town via NH-27. Take the road towards Bahadurganj market. Anti Lodge is near the main market area.
