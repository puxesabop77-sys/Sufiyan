🛡️ SAFETY RULES

Safety Guidelines for Students

🔒

Lock Your Room

Always lock your room when leaving. Keep your key safely. Report lost keys to management immediately.

🕙

Return Before 10 PM

Gate closes at 10 PM sharp. Always return before closing time. Inform management if you will be late due to emergency.

🚭

No Fire Hazards

No candles, lighters or open flames inside rooms. No cooking on electric coils. Avoid overloading sockets.

📱

Keep Phone Charged

Always keep your mobile phone charged for emergency communication. Save management number in your contacts.

🤝

Help Each Other

If you notice a fellow student in distress, inform management immediately. Look out for each other.

🚪

Authorized Entry Only

Do not allow unknown persons inside the premises. All visitors must register at the gate with management.

🏥 FIRST AID

What to Do in Common Emergencies

🤒

Fever / Illness

Inform management immediately. Nearest medical shop is within 5 minutes walk. Call 108 for ambulance if condition is serious. Do not ignore high fever.

🔥

Fire

Shout FIRE loudly, alert everyone. Leave the building using stairs immediately. Do NOT use elevator. Call 101. Assemble at the main gate. Never go back inside.

⚡

Power Outage

Do not touch any electrical switches or wires. Inform management. Use phone torch. Power backup arrangements will be made by management.

🦴

Injury / Accident

Apply basic first aid if possible. Call management on +91 9942346634 immediately. They will arrange transport to hospital. Call 108 for serious injuries.

🌪️

Natural Disaster

Stay indoors during storms. Move away from windows. Follow management instructions. Have emergency contact numbers ready on your phone.

In Any Emergency — Call Immediately! 🚨

Management is available 24/7 for student emergencies
