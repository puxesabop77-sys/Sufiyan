❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you want to know about Anti Lodge — answered.

What is the monthly rent at Anti Lodge? ▼
The monthly rent starts at ₹450 for triple sharing, ₹550 for double sharing, and ₹650 for a single room. All options include electricity, water and basic amenities.
Is Anti Lodge safe for students from villages? ▼
Yes, absolutely. Anti Lodge maintains 24/7 security and a responsible management team. Many students from surrounding villages stay here comfortably. Parents can contact the management anytime.
How do I book a room? ▼
Fill the online booking form on our website. Your details will be sent to our WhatsApp and our team will contact you within 24 hours to confirm availability.
What documents are needed for admission? ▼
A valid ID proof such as Aadhar Card, School/College ID, or any government-issued document is required. Guardian contact details are also collected.
What is the gate closing time? ▼
The gate closes at 10:00 PM daily. Students must return before this time. In case of emergency, inform management in advance.
Is there any security deposit? ▼
Yes, a refundable security deposit of ₹500 is collected at admission. It is returned after room inspection when you vacate.
Can I visit Anti Lodge before booking? ▼
Yes! You can visit in person anytime between 9 AM – 6 PM. Or contact us on WhatsApp to schedule a visit.
Are meals included in the rent? ▼
No, meals are not included. However, there are several affordable dhabas and canteens near Anti Lodge for breakfast, lunch and dinner.
Can a student leave mid-month? ▼
Yes, but a 15-day advance notice is required. Without notice, the security deposit may be forfeited. Prorated rent may apply.
How do I contact the management for emergencies? ▼
WhatsApp or call +91 9942346634 anytime. The team is available 24/7 for emergencies.
Is there a study room or common area? ▼
Yes, there is a shared common area where students can study in groups. Quiet hours are maintained throughout the premises.
What areas are close to Anti Lodge? ▼
Anti Lodge is located in Bahadurganj, close to coaching centres, schools, markets, medical shops and food stalls — everything a student needs.
