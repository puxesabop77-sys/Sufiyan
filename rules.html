📋 RULES

Rules & Guidelines

These rules ensure a safe, peaceful and productive environment for all students. Please read carefully before booking.

🕙

Entry Timing

Gate closes at 10:00 PM. All students must return before this time. Inform management in case of emergency stay-out.

🔇

Quiet Hours

Maintain silence between 10 PM – 6 AM. Respect others' study and sleep time.

🚭

No Smoking/Alcohol

Strictly no smoking, alcohol or any intoxicants inside the premises. Violation leads to immediate eviction.

👥

No Outsiders

Unauthorized visitors are not allowed inside rooms. Meet guests in the common area only.

🧹

Cleanliness

Keep your room and shared areas clean. Do not litter corridors or bathrooms.

💰

Rent Payment

Pay rent by the 5th of every month. Late payment may attract penalty. Advance payment preferred.

📢

Noise Discipline

No loud music, arguments or disturbances. Anti Lodge is a study-focused environment.

🔧

Property Care

Do not damage lodge property. Any damage will be charged to the student responsible.

📱

Emergency Contact

Always keep management informed about extended leaves or travel. Emergency numbers must be registered.

📋

ID Submission

Submit a valid ID proof (Aadhar/School ID) at the time of admission. Mandatory for all residents.

🤝

Respect Everyone

Treat fellow students, staff and management with respect. Ragging or bullying is strictly prohibited.

🚪

Vacating Notice

Give 15 days advance notice before vacating. No-notice departure may forfeit security deposit.

⚠️

Violation of rules may result in eviction without refund.

By booking a room at Anti Lodge, you agree to follow all the above rules.
