🏠 ABOUT US

Simple. Reliable. Student-Friendly.

Anti Lodge was born from one simple belief — every student deserves a safe, affordable place to call home while chasing their dreams.

OUR STORY

Why Anti Lodge Was Started

Every year, thousands of students from villages around Bahadurganj and Kishanganj come to town for coaching, school and competitive exam preparation. Many of them struggle to find affordable, safe accommodation.

Anti Lodge was created to solve this exact problem — provide clean, secure and affordable rooms so that students can fully focus on their education without financial burden or safety concerns.

We believe accommodation should never be a barrier to education. 🎓
